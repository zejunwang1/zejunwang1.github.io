Research   [top]

I received a B.S. in Information Engineering from South China University of Technology in 2014. When I was an undergrad, I worked with Prof. Li-Hua Guo on handwritten digit recognition and face recognition. I received a M.S. in Computational Biology from Tsinghua University in 2017. During my graduate study, I worked with Prof. Michael Q. Zhang on the statistical methods and computational model of three-dimensional chromatin structure. My research interests lie in natural language processing, social network community structure and text data mining. My github: https://github.com/zejunwang1
